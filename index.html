Project

Understand why we exisist and what we propose to solve

The aim of this research project is the development of an anti-theft system for power cables in junction boxes based on the Internet of Things (IoT). The system aims to reduce cable theft incidents and thereby contribute to improved quality in the availability of energy services. The project will be developed based on the investigation of anti-theft systems in junction boxes.
